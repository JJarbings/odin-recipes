Blueberrymuffins Recipe

Blueberrymuffin Origins

A muffin is an individually portioned baked product.
Sources suggest that "muffin" may be related to the Greek bread "maphula".
Muffins may have solid items mixed into the batter.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • backing powder
  • sugar
  • vanilla sugar
  • lemon zest
  • butter
  • eggs
  • milk
  • blueberries

Steps

  1. 200g flour 100g sugar 1 teaspoon lemon zest and backingpowder in bowl + mixing
  2. 100g butter melting
  3. mix melted butter with eggs and milk
  4. mix the buttermix with the ingredients in bowl
  5. mix washed blueberries into bowl
  6. mix rest of lemon zest with 50g sugar and vanilla sugar
  7. heat oven to 200 degrees
  8. mix everything into 12er muffinform
  9. bake muffins for 25 mins
